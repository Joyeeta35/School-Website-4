Campus Life

Cafetorium

Was established in 1973.

This is the Crescent Town cafetorium. It is a cafeteria, gymnasium and an auditorium. It is actually very big and very cool!

Playground

Was established in 1973.

This is the main Crescent Town playground. It has changed now, but I still remember the cool and big yellow swirl slide. It's just so cool!

School Fair

Started a few years ago.

This is the fun festival at our school. There are sweets, bubbles, toys, chips, snacks and even an ice-cream truck comes. There is cake and lots of clothing and fun!

Achievements

Platinum
Gold to Platinum!

Our school participated in the eco school program. A few other schools were at platinum while our school was at gold. All the students took care of the school and kept it clean. Later on, our school got platinum.

Best Principal Ever!

Our principal, Harpreet Ghuman was named one of Canada's most outstanding principals in 2019. He was an amazing principal and we had lots of fun with him. I think that he is the best principal ever!
